Who are we?

In 2017, a groundbreaking Esports organization emerged from the picturesque landscapes of Montenegro. Founded with a vision to redefine competitive gaming, this organization quickly gained recognition both domestically and internationally. With a dedicated team of professionals and a passion for gaming, they embarked on a journey that would shape the Esports scene in Montenegro.

From its inception, the organization prioritized fostering local talent and providing them with a platform to shine on the global stage. They scouted young, talented individuals who showcased exceptional skills in various Esports titles and brought them under the banner of their organization. With a solid roster of players, they ventured into tournaments and leagues, eager to make a mark.
